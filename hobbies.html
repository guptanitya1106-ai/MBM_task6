Hobbies?

To be very honest, I am someone who likes a variety of things to do at once but I'm not always the best at sticking to them. There are some constants though.

Let's start with my absolute favourite: dance. In particular, Bharatnatyam. This isn't to say that I don't enjoy western forms of dance. But it's meant to highlight the one that has stuck with me through all phases of my life.

An invite, from my arangetram which was one of the best days of my life. Not only was I performing my favourite dance pieces and feeling the music, but the realisation that I had so many people supporting me and my family, really made my day. So, as the music took over and my body danced to the rhythm, I experienced a high I had never before felt.

Next up, is basketball. Yes, it's been a while since I stepped foot on the court but again, its where I got my seconf fracture, shed countless tears and probably showed the most enthusiasm, running from one end of the court to the other. It taught me teamwork, companionship and ofcourse, allowing others to have control over the ball.

This is from the time we won the zonal championship in 9th grade.

Music has always been my go-to regardless of my mood-happy, sad, bored, nostalgic etc etc. Listening was never enough though. I learned the harmonium for almost 5 years as a kid. Then the bansuri and western flute and recorder as I entered middle school. I recently started learning the guitar. It itches some part of me that is untouched otherwise.

the only picture i have with my guitar, or any instrument really.

Other than these, I enjoy reading research papers for fun. Playing board games (especially Cluedo) has always been a favourite pass time of mine. Anything related to psychological mysteries or murder mysteries also intrigue me. Harry Potter also. I also like to travel and collect momentos from wherever I go. I like to unwind by doing watercolour paintings and recently tried adding salt which really made a difference. I prefer baking over cooking. Graphic design is also something I really enjoy.
